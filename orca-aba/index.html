Why Orca

Built for the families other providers turn down

Most ABA agencies in Utah focus narrowly on early intervention. We don't. From toddlers to young adults, mild cases to complex ones, we serve the families who've been told 'no' or 'we have a waitlist' too many times.

Ages 1 to 21

We serve children, teens, and young adults across the full developmental span — not just early intervention. School-age and adolescent kids deserve great ABA too.

Complex cases welcome

Severe behaviors, dual diagnoses, kids who've been declined or discharged elsewhere — we're built for the cases other providers turn down.

Mental health + ABA

When autism comes with anxiety, ADHD, depression, or trauma histories, we integrate behavioral mental health into our ABA practice — because one without the other often isn't enough.

25 years of experience

Founded and led by Dr. Lillian Adolphson, a doctorate-level BCBA with over 25 years building ABA programs. Every case has direct clinical oversight.

Meet our Founder

Dr. Lillian Adolphson

Founder & Chief Clinical Officer • Doctorate-Level BCBA

Lillian has spent the last 25 years in ABA — a doctorate-level Board Certified Behavior Analyst who has built and overseen clinical programs across the country. She founded Orca because she wanted to do ABA the way she always believed it should be done: individualized, family-first, and willing to take on the cases other agencies won't.

Every case at Orca has Dr. Adolphson's direct clinical oversight. Parents don't get handed off — they get a real conversation, however long it takes.
